WELCOME TO ST. V. N. PUBLIC SCHOOL

St. V. N. Public School, affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is located at Lakshagrih Road, Handia, Prayagraj. Our dream is that each and every child of this area can receive the education at a very nominal and affordable fees to get rid of ignorance and illiteracy.

DIRECTOR'S MESSAGE

We are pleased that you have taken the time to visit our website. We are committed to forster the growth and development of the children placed in our care. We aim to train them into value based, broad minded and creative children.
